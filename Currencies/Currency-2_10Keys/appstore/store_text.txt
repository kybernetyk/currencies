Features:
* Realtime Exchange Rates
* Over 70 Currencies
* New Currencies are added by support within an hour of notification
* Offline Mode
* Exchange Rate History

* Runs on iPhone OS 3 and 4 (and iPod Touch)

We believe we have made the most usable currency converter on the app store! We took all the feedback we got from our Currency.app users and rewrote the app from scratch. Now we believe we've come up with the best currency converter you can get. 

Our main concern was usability. It was most important to us to make the app as comfortable and as fast as possible to use for everyday users. Thus we implemented the amazing Quick Access Bookmarks which enable you to save often used conversion values for quick access with only one tap.


But that's not all. We've got a lot of feedback from people who travelled to regions of the world where network connections are really slow - and the roaming fees are incredibly high. So we built the forced offline mode which, no matter what, forces Currency 2 to stay offline and not even send one byte of data. In this mode Currency 2 does not update exchange rates and currency lists from the net - it uses saved exchange rates from the last time it was connected to the internet. Update the rates once in the morning over your hotel's wifi and do not care about roaming fees!

Then there's features like over 70 supported currencies, real time exchange rates, history graphs of exchange rates and an awesome support that will add any currency you think is missing - within hours of notification! 

Don't let fool you by other app's eye-candy which look nice but are simply unusable! Get Currency 2 now and never look for another currency converter!

Oh and yes: This app works on iPhone OS 3 and 4!
